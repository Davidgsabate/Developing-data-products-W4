Purpose of the app

  • With this calculator the user can plan the evolution of his weight loss or gain.
  • To do this, he must enter his current weight, target weight, and the date on which he wants to reach the goal.
  • A graph will be shown with the estimated forecast of the evolution, along with the number of calories he must lose (-), or gain (+) each day to reach the goal.

Plans length calculation

  • First, we need to calulate how many days the plan will take. In order to do so, we make some operations with the actual date and the user´s input.

Number of calories required and generation of the dataset with a linear model

  • To calculate the number of calories the user must gain or lose, we need to take into account the formula \[1Kg=7000KCal\]
